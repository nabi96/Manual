General questions to determine the mistake

  • Has the whole PC or just specific applications slowed down?
  • Does the machine feel slow when typing?
  • Do you have this problem in all cases, or only when certain programs are running?
  • Do you have the same lag if you start in safe mode?
  • Does the system start to slow after a few minutes of work or play, or is it slow from the start?
  • Is the system slow or also unstable?
  • are there any specific apps or apps that make this happen reliably?
